Easy clients

Easy Law does the hard yards to find your next client. We then make your life easy by collecting your fees upfront before you commence work so that you never have to waste your valuable time and resources chasing unpaid bills.

What's the deal?

We like simple things: simple agreements; simple terms and conditions; simple success for both lawyers and clients.

When a client engages you through our 'post a job' platform, Easy Law collects your quoted fees upfront and retains a 15% transaction fee. Easy Law then releases the remaining 85% to you upon completion of the job and takes care of the invoicing and other paperwork that takes the fun out of lawyering.
